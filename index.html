The Challenge: Manual Monitoring, High Risks

Manual reefer monitoring is prone to errors, time-consuming, and exposes your valuable cargo to significant risks. Inaccurate temperature settings, missed alerts, and inadequate data lead to costly claims and operational inefficiencies that impact your bottom line.

Incorrect Settings

Manual comparison of manifest and Baplie leads to critical temperature setting errors.

Improper Monitoring

Failure to highlight error codes results in large financial claims and cargo damage.

Inadequate Data

Lack of historical data comparison prevents comprehensive analysis at container locations.

Time-Consuming Tasks

Crew spends excessive time on manual data entry and report preparation daily.

Our Solution: Intelligent & Efficient Management

Automated Reefer Container Management

  • Seamlessly import manifests and Baplie files. Our app automates comparisons, instantly alerting you to discrepancies in temperature settings and manifest data, preventing costly errors.
  • Generate comprehensive daily and voyage reports with highlighted anomalies, drastically reducing crew workload and ensuring compliance.
  • Access critical container and cargo information on-site, anytime, anywhere.
  • Efficiently manage voyage creation and track reefer container spares inventory with automatic adjustments.

Intelligent Onboard NFC Patrols

  • Revolutionize security, fire, and weekly inspections with NFC-powered patrols. Utilize standard Android phones to scan checkpoints, verify attendance, and record inspection times effortlessly.
  • Easily import and update search locations via Excel files for streamlined patrol route management.
  • Ensure company procedures are rigorously followed with verifiable, time-stamped records, enhancing overall vessel security and compliance.
  • No additional handheld equipment required, reducing operational costs and complexity.
